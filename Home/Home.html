Mangrove Forest Facts

Mangrove forests/ swamps are forests on the coastal areas of cities. Mangrove forests are useful for many reasons. For exmple,

  • they are the most important carbon sinks(land that absorbs carbon) in the world
  • they acts as protection for floods, storms and tsunamis for the place they are at
  • their complex root systems clean rivers' water that is flowing into the sea
  • they give shelter to many rare animals in the world

In the past, despite their usefulness they were cut down to use as fuel or to get more spaces to plant other thinks (e.g-aquaculture, rice, and palm oil production). By doing this people are also destroying a big variety of rare animals on earth that will be mentioned in the animal tab on the navigation bar.
